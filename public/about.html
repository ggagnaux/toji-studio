About

I make abstract, cosmic-leaning digital artwork—geometry, atmosphere, and controlled chaos. This space is a slow gallery: minimal distractions, maximum presence.

Artist statement

My work lives between structure and drift—hard edges meeting soft fields, like constellations forming and dissolving. I’m interested in how simple systems can produce surprise.
