About

Hi, my name's Greg. I make abstract, cosmic-leaning digital artwork—geometry, atmosphere, and controlled chaos. This space is a slow gallery: minimal distractions, maximum presence.

Artist statement

My work lives between structure and drift—hard edges meeting soft fields, like constellations forming and dissolving. I’m interested in how simple systems can produce surprise.

If you’d like to license a piece, commission a variation, or talk about a custom backlit print, head to the contact page.

Process

The workflow is part design, part experiment. I start with a constraint, iterate until something clicks, then refine... and then refine some more. I like to let the work surprise me, and I often find the best ideas by pushing on a piece until it breaks, then seeing what’s left standing.
